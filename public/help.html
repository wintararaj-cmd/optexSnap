â“ Frequently Asked Questions

For Customers

Q: How do I track my order?

A: Go to the Orders page and enter your phone number or email to view all your orders and their current status.

Q: Can I cancel my order?

A: Yes, contact the restaurant immediately. Orders can be cancelled before they enter "Preparing" status.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: Cash on Delivery, Card Payment, UPI, and Net Banking (availability may vary).

For Admins

Q: How do I backup my data?

A: Use the Data Management section to export all data as JSON. Store this file safely and export regularly.

Q: How do I customize invoices?

A: Invoice templates can be customized in the Settings section under Invoice Configuration.

Q: Can I delete old orders?

A: Orders should be archived rather than deleted to maintain financial records. Use the archive feature in Order Management.
